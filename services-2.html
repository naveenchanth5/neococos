COIR PITH / COCO PEAT

Advantage of coir pith gardener

Coir Pith substrate is of natural origin and with high breeding properties
Coconut fiber and coco peat is one of the best substrates.
Good Ventilation: allows air circulation through the macrospores
Good water storage capacity: water is stored in the microspores of the substrate seven times of its volume.
pH neutral, suitable for most vegetables. High nutrient storage capacity that allows to exchange cation of iron, potassium, calcium etc.,
Its lightness offers lot of substrate with a low weight, thus not overload our growing pots and also for terrace gardens.
